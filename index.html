Questions?

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know about our painting services.

How long does a typical project take?

Most residential projects take 4-7 days depending on size, surface condition, and weather. Commercial spaces may take longer. We'll provide exact timeline after site inspection.

What paints do you use?

We exclusively use premium brands like Asian Paints, Berger, and Nerolac. All paints are eco-friendly and come with manufacturer warranty. We never compromise on paint quality.

Do you provide warranty on the work?

Yes! We provide 1-5 year warranty depending on the package selected. This covers paint peeling, color fading, and workmanship defects. Free touch-ups included within warranty period.

Can you work around my schedule?

Absolutely! We're flexible with timing. We can work early mornings, evenings, weekends, or as per your convenience. Our team ensures minimal disruption to your daily routine.

Is your quote binding?

Our quotes are binding with no hidden charges. What we quote is what you pay. If any issues are discovered during work, we discuss with you before proceeding with additional work.
